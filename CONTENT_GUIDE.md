# Content & Tone Guide

Guidelines to help craft site content for Lilly Combest:

- Audience: local clients in Pinehurst and remote clients interested in sustainable wellness.
- Tone: warm, professional, supportive, and evidence-informed.
- Homepage: succinct value statement, primary services, clear CTA to book a consult.
- About page: Lilly's background, approach, credentials, and a friendly photo.
- Services pages: clear descriptions, outcomes, who it's for, and pricing or inquiry CTA.
- Blog: short, helpful posts about nutrition, stress management, and practical wellness tips.

Use these files as the starting content and replace placeholders with Lily's actual copy and contact info.
